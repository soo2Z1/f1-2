Formula 1 Teams

Oracle Red Bull Racing

Is an Austrian auto racing team participating in the Formula 1 Championship . Winner of the Constructors' Cup in 2010 , 2011 , 2012 , 2013 , 2022, 2023.

Mercedes AMG Petronas

Is a German Formula One team and constructor of the Mercedes-Benz automobile concern , which has been participating in Formula One since the 2010 season . Eight-time winner of the Constructors' Cup in 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020 and 2021.

Scuderia Ferrari

Is the oldest and most legendary team in Formula 1. Founded in 1929 by Enzo Ferrari, the team has become a true symbol of passion, speed, and racing heritage. Ferrari holds the record for the most Constructors' Championships and has seen many of the greatest drivers in history behind the wheel.

McLaren F1 Team

Is one of the most iconic and successful teams in Formula 1 history. Founded in 1963 by Bruce McLaren, the team has a rich legacy, including multiple championship titles, legendary drivers, and unforgettable cars.

Aston Martin Aramco

Is a historic name in motorsport that made its full-time return to Formula 1 as a works team in 2021. Since then, the team has been steadily building momentum, aiming to challenge for podiums and victories.

Williams Racing

Is one of the most iconic and respected teams in Formula 1 history. Founded in 1977 by Sir Frank Williams and Patrick Head, the team became a dominant force in the 1980s and 1990s, winning multiple Constructors’ and Drivers’ Championships.

Haas F1 Team

Is an American Formula 1 team founded by businessman Gene Haas in 2016. It became the first full-time F1 team from the United States in decades and has been a consistent presence on the grid ever since.

Stake F1 Team Kick Sauber

Is the current name of the Swiss-based team formerly known as Alfa Romeo. With deep roots in motorsport, Sauber has been a part of Formula 1 since 1993, and today the team is preparing for a major transformation — becoming the Audi works team in 2026.

RB Formula 1 Team

Is an Italian Formula One racing team . It debuted in the 2024 season . It is one of two Formula One constructors owned by Austrian conglomerate Red Bull GmbH , the other being Red Bull Racing . Known as Scuderia AlphaTauri from the 2020 season to the 2023 season , the team was renamed to its current name for the 2024 season.

BWT Alpine F1 Team

Is a French team that emerged as the rebranded successor of the legendary Renault F1 Team. It officially entered Formula 1 under the Alpine name in 2021, representing the sporty sub-brand of Renault known for its performance cars and racing spirit.
